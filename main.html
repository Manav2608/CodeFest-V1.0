Cooperative Learning

Cooperative learning is a teaching method that groups students into pairs or small groups where they are challenged to complete a task.

Cooperative Learning

Successful completion of a task is determined how well the pair or group work together. This teaching method promotes peer-to-peer respect and develops understanding of each others interests, skills and needs.

The Play Method

An ideal kindergarten classroom is a safe environment that provides ample opportunity for children to play, which in turn develops confidence and social skills.

Know More

More
About The Play Method

The first step to play as a teaching method is observation -- then the teacher can structure the play. Teachers should do so without dominating or directing the play, but by sharing the experience with the children and presenting ideas and materials that will help extend the play into a learning experience.
